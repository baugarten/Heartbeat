download music, free

Tired of paying too much for music, giving your money away to record labels? At Heartbeat you can download music for free and donate if you like them. The artist gets 90% of everything you donate!

Your time should be spent recording music and connecting with fans. We make that easy. You can update fans, ask for feedback, figure out where to tour, fund an album, and more! If you can't do it, just ask us to make it a reality!
